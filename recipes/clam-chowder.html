Clam chowder

  • Home Page

Description

Simple, creamy clam chowder loaded with potatoes and bacon. Starting with a dry soup mix and half-and-half makes the recipe super easy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pund bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 5 unpeeled potatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 (6.5 oz) cans chopped clams
  • 2 (1.8 oz) packages dry leek soup mix
  • 1 quart half-and-half
