Spicy Shrimp and Kidney Beans

  • Home Page

Description

Shrimp, red beans (or kidney), tomatoes with chiles, and cream of mushroom soup make this soup an easy-to-make crowd pleaser that looks and tastes like soup from a fine restaurant. Serve with Garlic Toast to make it a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 medium celery rib, chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 (15 oz) can of red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (10 oz) can tomatoes with green chillies
  • 1 (10 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 sprig fresh dill
  • 12 ounces medium shrimp - peeled and deveined

Instructions

  1. Warm oil in large saucepan
  2. Stir in garlic, celery and green onions. Cook until tender, about 3 minutes
  3. Stir in beans, tomatoes, cream of mushroom soup, and water. Bring to boil.
  4. Stir in dill, reduce heat to medium, and simmer 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in shrimp, and simmer until shrimp are pink, about 5 minutes.
